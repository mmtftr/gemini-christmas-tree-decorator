3D Christmas Tree Decorator

What is it?

An interactive 3D web app that lets you:

  • Decorate a beautiful 3D Christmas tree
  • Generate live Christmas music with AI
  • Choose from multiple festive themes
  • Share your creation with friends
  • Collaborate in real-time via Convex sync

Beautiful 3D Interface

Themes panel with multiple festive options
